sweeping adj

1、决定性的，深远的have sweeping effects具有决定性的影响
sweeping changes意义深远的变化

2、全面的，总括的a sweeping victory全胜
a sweeping statement总括性的说法

eg:Two weeks after America withdrew from a nuclear deal with Iran,Mike Pomeo,America’s secrtary of state,called for a more sweeping agreement.
翻译：在美国退出与伊朗核协议两周后，美国国务卿麦克彭斯呼吁更全面的协议

appoint vt
1、任命，委派 They appointed him manager 他们任命他为经理
They appointed him to the post他们委派他担任该职
2、确定，决定Let’s appoint a time for the meeting 我们确定一下开会时间吧，
3、装备，布置The room is comfortably appointed这个房间布置很舒适。
eg:Your country needs you they ltaly’s president appointed
a non-politican,Giuseppe Conte,as prime minitster.

翻译：你的国家你的需要意大利总统任命非政治人士作为总理

cabinet n 内阁

1、a cabinet meeting内阁会议
A Cabinet reshuffle 内阁改组 a memeber of the cabinet内阁成员

2、储藏柜，陈列柜 a kitchen cabinet 碗橱
a china cabinet 瓷器陈列柜

eg:Mr Conte faces the ticklish(棘手的) task of trying to run a cabinet including the leaders of the two parties that will
make up the new populist government :Luigi Di.
翻译：肯特面临一项棘手的任务组建一个包括两党领导人

populist adj
民粹主义的

eg:Mr Conte faces the ticklish task of trying to run a cabinet including the leaders of the two parties that will
make up the new populist government :Luigi Di

ticklish adj
1、怕痒的
2、非正式的，棘手的，难处理的 a ticklish situation 难应付的局面
Mr Conte faces the ticklish task of trying to run a cabinet including the leaders of the two parties that will
make up the new populist government :Luigi Di
肯特先生面临棘手的组阁任务，包括两党的领导人组成新的民粹政府。

household 一户人家
eg：The Swedish government began sending leaflets to every
household advising Swedes what to do if war breaks out.
翻译：瑞士政府开始发宣传单给没一户建议瑞士人怎么做如果战争爆发。
cope

1、应付，处理cope with a new situation 应付新局面
cope with difficulties(设法)克服困难
2、竞争，争斗No one can cope with her in English.
在英语方面，谁也比不上她。
cope with 有成功克服的意思
deal wih 克服，对付，应付

eg：It included tips on how to cope if food and water are short,or if electricity,phones and computer systems fail.
翻译：这里包括如何应对食物和水短缺或者电力，电话，网络系统瘫痪。
literature n
1、文学作品；文学
2、著述，文献 the literature on particular
subject有关某个学科的文献
3、小册子，宣传品 sales literature 推销产品的宣传材料

eg:Such literature has not been distributed to the public
since 1961,during the cold war.

leaflet
1、嫩叶，小叶
2、活页，传单

eg：The Swedish government began sending leaflets to every
household advising Swedes what to do if war breaks out.

distributed
1、分配，分给
The prizes were distributed among five winners.

2、分布，散布 distribut manure over a field 把肥料撒在田里

3、区分，把…分类
These plants are distributed into 22 classes.这些植物分为22类。

vi
1、分发，分配
2、散布，分布

eg：Such literature has not been distributed to the public since 1961,during the cold war.

2、暂令…停止；暂时剥夺…的权利
3、正式悬挂
4、使悬浮
5、延缓；暂缓执行 suspend a decision 暂缓做出决定

eg：He was suspended in 2016 after a row over allegedly anti-Semtic comments he made.
